About

I'm Ro Dixon, UX Designer by day and Web developer by night. I've spent the last seven years in UX and I love it, but I found myself tired of turning to a developer every time one of my designs was ready to produce. So I've been learning to code my designs myself, and it's been liberating. Now I really want to share that ability with others.
